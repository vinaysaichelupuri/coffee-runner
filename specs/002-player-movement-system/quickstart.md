# Quickstart: player-movement-system

## Testing the implementation

1. Launch the app using `npm start`.
2. Swipe left and right on the GameScreen.
3. Observe the player component smoothly animating between the three fixed lane positions.
4. Attempt to swipe left from the leftmost lane and verify the player does not move off-screen.
5. Attempt to swipe right from the rightmost lane and verify the player does not move off-screen.
